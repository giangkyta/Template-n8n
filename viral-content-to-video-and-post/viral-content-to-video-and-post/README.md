# Viral content to video and auto post (n8n)

This template collects viral content ideas, generates videos, and publishes posts automatically using n8n.

## What this workflow does
- Collects viral content ideas
- Turns ideas into videos using external video APIs
- Publishes posts on a schedule
- Uses Google Sheets to manage content status
